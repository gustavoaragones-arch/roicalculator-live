Frequently asked questions

How does gross benefit differ from net annual benefit here?

Gross benefit is the annual upside from throughput, labor, or margin; net annual benefit subtracts incremental operating cost like maintenance, consumables, or service contracts tied to the asset.

Does this include financing cost?

No—capex is treated as cash cost at purchase. Add interest if you want a levered view, or use a lease-versus-buy workbook for full capital structure.

How does this compare to payback period?

Payback is time to recover investment; this ROI is net annual benefit divided by upfront capex for one representative year. Extend with multi-year cash flows for long-lived assets.
