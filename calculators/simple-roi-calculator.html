Frequently asked questions

What is a simple ROI calculation?

A simple ROI calculation measures profit relative to cost without considering time or compounding.

When should you use a simple ROI?

Use it for quick comparisons or when the time factor is not important.

What are the limitations of simple ROI?

It ignores time value of money and may mislead for long-term investments.
