Frequently asked questions

How do you calculate ROI with an example?

Subtract the initial investment from the final value, divide by the initial investment, and multiply by 100.

Why use examples in ROI calculations?

Examples help clarify how the formula works in real-world scenarios.

What does a 50% ROI mean?

It means the investment generated half of its original value as profit.
