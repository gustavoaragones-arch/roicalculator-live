Frequently asked questions

Is influencer marketing ROI measurable with orders and AOV?

Yes for trackable promo or code-attributed orders: multiply order count by average order value for attributed sales, subtract the fee, then divide by the fee for ROI. Untrackable lift requires modeled incrementality instead.

Should I include product cost in influencer ROI?

Gross sales produce one story; contribution after COGS produces another. This model uses sales minus fee as “profit”—add COGS externally if you need margin-accurate ROI.

How is this different from MER?

Marketing efficiency ratio divides total revenue by total ad spend across channels. This page is deal-level for influencer fees versus modeled attributed sales from orders × AOV.
