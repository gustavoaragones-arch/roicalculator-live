Privacy

roicalculator.live is designed with privacy as a core principle. This page explains what we do and do not do with your information.

No Data Collection

We do not collect personal data. We do not require accounts, email addresses, or any identifiable information to use the site. The ROI calculator runs entirely in your browser. Numbers you enter are never transmitted to our servers because the site does not use a backend that receives user input.

No Cookies

We do not set cookies. The site does not use first-party or third-party cookies for tracking, analytics, or advertising.

No Local Storage

We do not use localStorage, sessionStorage, or similar browser storage for user data. Your calculator inputs and results are not persisted by the site.

No Analytics or Tracking

We do not embed analytics scripts (e.g., Google Analytics, Mixpanel) or tracking pixels. We do not track page views, clicks, or user behavior.

Third-Party Resources

The site loads Chart.js from a CDN (jsDelivr) to render the projection chart. That request is subject to the CDN providerâ€™s policies. We do not load advertising or social sharing scripts.

Hosting and Logs

If the site is hosted by a third party, that host may collect standard server logs (IP address, access time, requested URLs). We do not control or use those logs for tracking. We encourage hosts to minimize log retention.

Changes

If we change this privacy policy, we will update this page and the effective date. A privacy-first approach will remain central to the site.

Summary: We do not collect, store, or share your personal data. No cookies. No tracking.
